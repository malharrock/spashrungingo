Agricultural guidance and water conservation programmes

Shree Saptshrungi Education Society Chandrapur provides guidance on agriculture. Farmers are guided to know the fertility of their land and grow crops accordingly. Also, the farmers are guided by the agricultural experts on how to grow three crops in a year.

Also different crops should be grown instead of taking only one traditional crop and farmers should cultivate seasonal crops so farmers can get good price guarantee and traditional fertilizers should be used in agriculture and chemical fertilizers should not be used. Also, the organization regularly gives various guidance to the farmers that they should not spray chemical liquids.

Also, various guidance programs are conducted by the organization for farmers on how to use drip irrigation, how to create water ponds in agriculture and how to conserve water in agriculture. Excellent farmers are always honored by the organization.



Environment protection and tree plantation programme.

Environmental protection and tree plantation programmes are regularly implemented by Shree Saptshrungi Education Society Chandrapur."One focus let's plant eleven hundred trees every year" "Let's live the earth".

The main reason why we are seeing various changes in the environment and climate today is due to increasing pollution and numerous deforestation.Due to this, the environment of our earth is very damaged and there are various changes in the environment. To maintain the balance of the environment, it is very necessary for all of us to plant trees together. For that, 11000 trees are regularly planted by our organization every year. In tree plantation programmes, School students, parents, wise citizens of the society, family members are given the guardianship of the tree. If the trees planted by all of them survive for two years continuously, then after two years those whose trees have survived are honored by the organization with a certificate of appreciation.

ASaving trees and planting trees is the need of the hour so everyone in the society should participate in this environment protection and tree plantation programmes and everyone should support us in this campaign to save our earth.



Cleanliness Campaign and Cleanliness Awareness Programme.

Swachhta Abhiyan is regularly implemented by Shree Saptshrungi Education Society Chandrapur and also Swachhata awareness programmes are conducted regularly. If our environment is clean, our health will also be good, our village, city and country will also look beautiful. Various programmes are conducted by the organization, in this school and college students are guided about cleanliness and the importance of cleanliness have been told.

Also, by going to various departments and carrying out a cleanliness campaign, the locality is cleaned and the help of various people in the society is taken in this programme. Also, the people of the society are told about the importance of cleanliness and the organization participates in the cleanliness campaign implemented by the Government of India and the Government of Maharashtra



Women Training and Self Employment Programme.

Shree Saptshrungi Education Society Chandrapur conducts various training programmes for women to empower women. With the aim of creating self-employment for women, the organization is trying to provide employment to women in various ways such as cake making, beauty parlour training, anganwadi maids training, home industry training and so on.

Also, women are regularly felicitated by the organization on International Women's Day. In order to create employment for women, efforts are always made by the organization to make them stand on their own feet through business training etc.
